About BTSAnneyong!!!

BTS also known as Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band that began formation in 2010 and debuted in 2013 under Bighit Entertainment.The septet, which is composed of RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook, co-writes and co-produces much of the Groups Output.Their lyrics often focused on personal and social commentary, touch on the themes of mental health, troubles of school-age youth, loss, the journey towards loving oneself, and individualism. The group has released several albums and performed on several world tours.
